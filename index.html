Photo Converter

Technologies used:
Python, OpenCV, DLIB

This was a part of our college's own project, and I had the opportunity to contribute in it. It mainly converts the existing colour images (24 bit) into a new greyscale image (8 bit) without decreasing quality of the actual photo.
